This page will go over some fun facts about Kentucky's largest cities, including the capital Frankfort.

Before getting started, here are a few lists of things that you may not know about Kentucky:
  • Kentucky's largest farm income comes from horses, broilers, cattle, tobacco, soybeans, and corn.
  • Kentucky is one of the leading beef cattle states east of the Mississippi River.
  • 50% of Kentucky's land is commercial forest land (and there are many parks to talk about for each city!).

Did you know? Abraham Lincoln was born in Hodgenville, KY!
